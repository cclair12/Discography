BIOGRAPHY

Sundy Best is an American country duo formed by Nick Jamerson and Kris Bentley, who are both from Prestonburg, Kentucky. Their music is a blend of a few genres, including Appalachian folk, country, rock, and soul. The name of the duo originates from the way that they pronounce “Sunday Best”, which is their best clothing worn when playing in Church on Sundays during high school.

The two have been friends since elementary school and set out to perform at an early age. They played music in church until eventually forming their own band in high school. They started out playing bar gigs in Lexington, Kentucky and released their first official album in 2012, which increased their popularity tremendously and led to a multi-year deal with Entertainment One label group.
